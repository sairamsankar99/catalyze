Powerful Features

Everything you need for fast, accurate field inspections — powered by the latest AI vision models.

📸

Instant AI Analysis

Snap a photo of any component and get an instant PASS, MONITOR, or FAIL assessment powered by GPT-4o Vision. Includes confidence scores and actionable maintenance steps.

🎙️

Voice Inspections

Go hands-free in the field. Speak your observations naturally and let AI classify component health, generate reports, and recommend next steps — all from voice alone.

📊

Health Analytics

Track machine health across sessions with interactive donut charts, bar graphs, and timeline trends. Monitor fleet-wide equipment degradation before breakdowns happen.

🎥

Live Inspection Mode

Point your camera at equipment and get real-time AI assessments every 8 seconds. Walk around the machine while CATalyze continuously identifies and evaluates components.

How It Works

Three simple steps from field to report. No training required.

1

Select Machine

Choose your CAT equipment model. CATalyze loads every component that needs inspection for a complete walkaround.

2

Inspect Components

Capture photos or speak observations for each component. AI returns instant condition assessments with step-by-step maintenance guidance.

3

Generate Report

One tap produces a color-coded report with voice narration, analytics dashboard, and a downloadable PDF summary for your records.
