Features

Everything you need for fast, accurate field inspections.

📸

Instant AI Analysis

Snap a photo of any component. GPT-4o Vision identifies condition and returns PASS, MONITOR, or FAIL in seconds.

🎙️

Voice Inspections

Speak your observations hands-free. AI interprets your voice notes and classifies component health automatically.

📊

Health Analytics

Track machine health scores over time with interactive charts, trend lines, and historical comparison across sessions.

🎥

Live Inspection Mode

Point your camera and get real-time AI assessments every 3 seconds. No buttons to press — just walk and inspect.

How It Works

Three simple steps from field to report.

1

Select Machine

Choose your equipment model. CATalyze loads every component that needs inspection for that machine.

2

Inspect Components

Capture photos or speak observations for each component. AI returns instant condition assessments with maintenance steps.

3

Generate Report

One tap produces a full color-coded report with voice narration, analytics dashboard, and downloadable summary.

About CATalyze

Built for field inspectors who need speed and accuracy. CATalyze combines GPT-4o Vision, voice recognition, and real-time analytics to transform heavy equipment walkaround inspections into a streamlined, AI-assisted workflow. No training required — just point, shoot, and go.
